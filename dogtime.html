Samoyed

The Samoyed is a breed of medium-sized herding dogs with thick, white, double-layer coats.
The Samoyed dog breed, originally bred to hunt, haul sledges, and herd reindeer, proved a valuable
companion for the Samoyede people of northwestern Siberia. The dogs also hiked with their packs,
tracked, and warmed their owners by sleeping on top of them at night.

Golden Retriever

The Golden Retriever is a Scottish breed of retriever dog of medium size. It is characterised by a gentle
and affectionate nature and a striking golden coat. It is also used as a gun dog (a type of hunting dog
for soft-mouthed retrieving of fowl) and may be trained for use as a guide dog.
